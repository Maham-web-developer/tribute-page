The missible mam of india

Here's a time line of Dr. A p J Abdul kalam

  • 1931: Born Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam on 15 October , in Ramesawaram ,TamiNadu.
  • 1980: India enters the space club by putting the Rohini Statellite in the near orbit with the first indigenous Statelline launds Vehicle (SLVIII), developed under the stewardship of Dr. Kalam
  • 1980-1990: As the Chief of the Integrate Guided Missile Development , he wa responsible for the Development and operationalisation of AGNI and PRITHVD Missiles.
  • 1992-1999: Works as Chief Scientific Advisor to the PM and Secretory of the DRDO.
  • 1998: India Conducts the Pokhran II nuclear test in May with Dr. Kalam as the Chief project coordinate.
  • 1999-2001: Principle Scientific Advisior to the Government of India.
  • 2002-2007: president of India
